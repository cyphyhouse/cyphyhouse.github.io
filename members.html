PIs

Sayan Mitra

Sayan Mitra is a Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. His research interests are in formal verification, distributed computing, and cyberphysical systems.

Geir Dullerud

Geir Dullerud is the W. Grafton and Lillian B. Wilkins Professor in the department of Mechanical Science and Engineering in the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. His areas of current research interest include control theory, cyber-physical systems security and privacy, games, robotic vehicles, and hybrid dynamical systems.

Student researchers

Ritwika Ghosh

Ritwika is a CS graduate student, interested in programming language semantics and formal verification of distributed systems. She is working on the design and formal semantics of high level language, and formal verification of applications developed using this framework.
Joao Porto

Joao is a MechSE graduate student, interested in controls research for decentralized systems. He is currently designing a fleet of quadrotors and a indoor poisitioning sytem based on ultra-wideband technology.

Amelia Gosse

Amelia is a senior undegraduate researcher with CyPhyHouse, interested in controls research including MPC and PID control. She is currently working on developing an optimal path planning system and sophisticated controllers for the F1/10th platform .

Chiao Hsieh

Chiao is a PhD student in CS department. He is generally interested in program analysis, formal verification, and testing of distributed protocols. His work is to translate the high level language to executable Python program as well as test and integrate different components in the framework.

Minghao Jiang

Minghao is a senior Computer Engineering student and will become an ECE graduate student next semester. He is interested in computer software and autonomous system. He is working on the simulator that provides the visualization of robotic vehicles.

John Wang

John is an undergraduate researcher in CyPhyHouse working on developing a robust compiler with intermediate representations to enable code optimization and program analysis. His research interests include programming languages and compilers.

Zitong Chen

Zitong is an undergraduate researcher in CyPhyHouse working on device discovery and launch protocols for deployment of distributed applications on heterogenous platforms. His research interests include machine learning, IoT and networking and security.
