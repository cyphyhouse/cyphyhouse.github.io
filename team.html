PIs

Sayan Mitra

ECE

Sayan Mitra is a Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. His research interests are in formal verification, distributed computing, and cyberphysical systems.

Geir Dullerud

MECHSE

Geir Dullerud is the W. Grafton and Lillian B. Wilkins Professor in the department of Mechanical Science and Engineering in the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. His areas of current research interest include control theory, cyber-physical systems security and privacy, games, robotic vehicles, and hybrid dynamical systems.

Student Researchers

Ritwika Ghosh

CS

Ritwika is a graduate student, interested in programming languages and formal methods. She is developing the Koord language and  CyPhyHouse middleware. She is also working on the formal verification of applications developed using this framework.

Joao Porto

MECHSE

Joao is a graduate student, interested in controls research for decentralized systems. He is currently designing a fleet of quadrotors and a indoor positioning sytem based on ultra-wideband technology.

Amelia Gosse

MECHSE

Amelia is a senior undegraduate researcher with CyPhyHouse, interested in controls research including MPC and PID control. She is working on developing an optimal path planning system and sophisticated controllers for the F1/10th platform .

Chiao Hsieh

CS

Chiao is a graduate student, generally interested in program analysis, formal verification, and testing of distributed protocols. His work is to translate the high level language to executable Python programs as well as test and integrate different components in the framework.

Minghao Jiang 

ECE

Minghao is a graudate student interested in computer software and autonomous systems. He is working on the simulator that provides the visualization of robotic vehicles, as well as pedestrian detection.
